Welcome to Simple Platformer

I am a student studying at Bedfordshire University, and I am working on this game as my end-of-year project. The reason I chose to develop this game is to apply the knowledge I've gained in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript over the years.

The game I'm creating aims to provide educational benefits to young primary school kids. By incorporating fun gameplay elements, it offers a engaging way for children to enhance their studies while having fun.
