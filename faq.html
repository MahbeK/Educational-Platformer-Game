Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What inspired you to create this game?

A: I was inspired to create this game as part of my end-of-year project at Bedfordshire University. Drawing from my knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I wanted to develop a fun and educational game for young primary school kids. The goal is to provide an interactive learning experience that combines gameplay with educational benefits.
